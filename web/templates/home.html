    Kappa Sigma Gamma Pi Chapter

    MASSACHUSETTS INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY

    For more than a century, Kappa Sigma has shaped the lives of hundreds of MIT men, creating leaders on campus and in the world beyond.

    Located conveniently on West Campus between Burton Connor and Baker House, KΣ is placed right in the heart of MIT campus, along with several of its fraternal neighbors.

    Members of KΣ are involved with many different aspects of life on campus. Whether that be as players on MIT's sports teams, members and leaders of student clubs on campus, or just another friendly face in class.
